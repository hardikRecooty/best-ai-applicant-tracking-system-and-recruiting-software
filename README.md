## **What Are Common ATS Troubleshooting Issues?**

Software is never completely perfect always. Recruiters sometimes face frustrating technical issues. Let us fix these common problems.

### **Issue 1: Candidates Are Not Applying**

You posted a great job online. But your ATS shows zero applicants. This is a very scary problem.

**Step-by-step fix:**

1. Check your job board API connections.  
2. Review your application form length.  
3. Test the mobile application experience yourself.

If the form has fifty questions. \-\> Then candidates will abandon it immediately. If you ask for just resumes. \-\> Then application rates will skyrocket fast.

Preventive tip: Keep applications incredibly short. Ask for detailed info later on.

### **Issue 2: Automated Emails Are Failing**

Candidates say they receive no emails. Your interview invites are getting lost. Your employer brand looks very bad.

**Step-by-step fix:**

1. Check your ATS email trigger settings.  
2. Verify your company email domain reputation.  
3. Look in your own spam folder.

If your triggers are turned off. \-\> Then the ATS sends absolutely nothing. If you activate the right triggers. \-\> Then communication flows very smoothly again.

Preventive tip: Always test emails internally first. Send a dummy application to yourself.
